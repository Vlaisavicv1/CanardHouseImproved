Volunteer

Our work is made possible by people like you - those willing to show up, share their skills, and make a difference. Your time can change lives. Be part of the impact and join our volunteer community today!

Digital Literacy Tech Lab Volunteer

Help us bridge the digital divide and volunteer in our Tech Lab, where Conard House community members can come to learn basic to intermediate device skills, receive 1:1 individualized help, and build up their digital literacy.

Food Distribution at Supportive Housing Sites

Join Conard House in making a real difference by helping distribute nutritious meals to residents at our supportive housing sites. Volunteers assist with organizing, delivering, and handing out food, ensuring everyone has access to healthy options. Your time helps build community, support those in need, and bring smiles to residents who rely on these vital resources.

Corporate collaborations

Are you and your organization interested in volunteering with us? Our corporate collaborations are an excellent opportunity for external partners to get involved with our mission.

Supportive Housing to Empower People

Canard House is a nonprofit organization that offers housing to over 725 adults across 10 residential buildings and 22 shared apartments. Supporting those in deep poverty, our mission is to build welcoming communities and caring relationships that empower people and restore hope. Our core belief is that everyone can and should have a place to call home. A place to feel safe secure and comfortable. We must ensure that everyone has a place to call home in San Francisco, come see how you can help and help us make it happen.

We Bring Results

We have helped thousands across the San Francisco area find a way out of poverty and into stability. Our financial stability programs provide help to people whome might have as little as eleven dollars a day to provide for themselves.
